Routine Success

Routine Success is an app for the Apple Watch that can bring routines to aid a child in staying focused and organized, particularly at school.

Routine Success allows parents to build a virtual schedule for a child’s day.

The smartwatch then vibrates gently to prompt the child to take particular actions throughout the day.

Routine Success

Children with ASD need a routine to follow, crave a repetitive structure, and may have very high anxiety and fear of attending school.

Kids with neurological conditions like ADHD and High-Functioning Autism tend to do well with regular routines and sometimes need reminders

While Mom & Dad can serve that purpose at home, a busy teacher can’t really fill that role at school

Routine Success

I developed Routine Success because I saw firsthand how regular routines can help young people with conditions like Autism Spectrum Disorder and ADHD be more productive, confident, and independent throughout the day.
