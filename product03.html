iSocial

iSocial is a special needs virtual learning environment which will significantly help children with ASD develop their social skills.

Over the past 7 years and with $12m in grants, The University of Missouri developed a 32 intervention program called iSocial.

The program is evidence based and 7 independent university studies show “significant” improvement in helping children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).
