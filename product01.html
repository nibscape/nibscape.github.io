PANDA

PANDA provides a simple way for pediatricians to screen for the risk of developmental disorders, including Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), in children as young as three months of age.

There are two patents on a device that uses near infrared to measure how a pupil reacts to a flashing light.

Extensive lab tests at the Thompson Center for Autism shows high accuracy in detecting autism as early as six months in age.
