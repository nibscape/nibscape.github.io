iSocial Adventures

A library of web-based interventions for parents: VLE, animations, short-form video with parent guides and real-time communication

The iSocial Home Adventures program for parents and students will offer brief games and exercises presented in a 3D world.

These games and exercises will allow students to independently explore and learn while maintaining a relationship with their avatar.
